Assignment 3 - Average Monthly Temperature by State Dashboard

This dashboard presents two visualizations that provide insights into the average monthly temperatures across various states in the United States.

1. Map-Based Visualization

The map-based visualization displays the average monthly temperatures for each state, with colors representing different temperature ranges. This geographic representation helps users quickly identify regional temperature patterns and anomalies. The map allows for an intuitive understanding of how temperatures vary across different parts of the country, which is especially useful for spotting geographical trends and regional climate differences.

2. Temperature Trend Over Time (Line Chart)

The line chart shows the trend of average monthly temperatures over the years for each state. This visualization allows users to track temperature changes over time, providing insights into long-term climate trends and potential patterns of warming or cooling. By including geographic information (states) as a color dimension, the chart makes it easy to compare temperature trends between different states.
