Assignment 4 - Overall Scores by State Dashboard

This dashboard presents visualizations that provide insights into the overall scores across various states in the United States. The overall score is calculated based on several key factors, including:

  • Fatal crash rate per 100K drivers
  • Fatal crash rate involving a drunk driver per 100K drivers
  • Fatal crash rate involving a drowsy driver per 100K drivers
  • DUI arrests per 100K drivers
  • Percentage of uninsured drivers

These metrics are combined to generate a comprehensive safety score, reflecting the overall driver safety in each state.

Top 10 Worst Driving States

This bar chart compares the overall satisfaction scores of different states, highlighting the top 10 states with the worst driving scores.

Approach: To create this visualization, I used Tableau to aggregate the comprehensive satisfaction scores for each state. The data was then represented in a horizontal bar chart format to facilitate easy comparison. The bars were color-coded to enhance visual distinction between the states, and the x-axis was formatted to display the scores as decimals for precision.

Top 10 Worst Driving States (Misleading)

This bar chart also compares the average overall satisfaction scores of different states, but it has been intentionally manipulated to mislead the viewer by truncating the x-axis.

Approach: In this visualization, the x-axis is truncated, starting from 0.60 instead of 0. This exaggerates the differences between the states, making the variations in scores appear more significant than they actually are. This technique can mislead viewers into thinking there are larger discrepancies in the data than truly exist.

Why this is bad: Misleading visualizations like this can distort the audience's perception of the data. By exaggerating differences, they can create a false sense of urgency or severity that does not accurately reflect reality. This can lead to incorrect conclusions, misinformed decisions, and a loss of trust in the data presented. Ensuring accurate and honest representation of data is crucial for maintaining integrity and reliability in data-driven storytelling.
